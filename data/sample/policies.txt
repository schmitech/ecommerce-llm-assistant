# TechGear Store Policies

## Shipping Policy
- Free domestic shipping on orders over $50
- International shipping available to 150+ countries
- International shipping costs:
  * Standard (10-15 business days): $15-30
  * Express (3-5 business days): $35-50
- Import duties and taxes are buyer's responsibility
- All orders include tracking information
- Insurance included on orders over $200

## Return Policy
- 30-day return window for unopened items with original packaging and receipt
- 14-day return window for opened items in resalable condition
- Defective items can be returned within 30 days for full refund or replacement
- Return shipping is free for defective items
- Other returns require customer to pay return shipping
- Refunds are processed within 5 business days to original payment method
- Items must be returned with all accessories and documentation

## Warranty
- 1-year standard warranty on all products
- Extended warranty available for purchase
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects
- Software issues not covered by warranty
