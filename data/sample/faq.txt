# Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 30-day return policy for all unopened items. Opened items must be returned within 14 days.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, we ship to select countries. International shipping takes 7-14 business days.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: You can track your order using the tracking number sent to your email after shipping.

Q: Do you offer warranty?
A: All products come with a 1-year standard warranty. Extended warranty options are available.
